The Coaches

Our Coaches

The Suffern Mounties Baseball Coaches are highly accomplished mentors, shaping champions with passion and expertise.
Their dedication to player development and baseball inspires success both on and off the pitch.

Our Coaches

Come meet the coaches, the ones that pull the team together to win.

Head Coach

Mike Cronin is the dedicated head of the Suffern baseball team. With his expertise and passion for the sport, he has transformed the team into a formidable force. Coach Cronin's leadership inspires his players to excel, fostering both skill development and a strong team dynamic with the help of his assistants, Wayne Poppe and JR Randazzo. His commitment has made him an invaluable asset to the Suffern baseball team.

Varsity

The Suffern High School varsity team is renowned for their exceptional performance and dedication. With skilled athletes and experienced coaches, they consistently demonstrate outstanding skill, teamwork, and determination. As role models for aspiring athletes, their achievements make them a source of pride for the Suffern community.

Junior Varsity

Led by Head Coach Nick Kulbaba and assisted by Chris Michie and Artie Connolly, the Suffern High School junior varsity (JV) team showcases dedication, teamwork, and continuous improvement. Under their guidance, these skilled athletes excel and lay the foundation for future success.

Head Coach: Mike Cronin

Named only the 5th Varsity Head Coach in the program’s history. Coach Cronin lives in Ramsey, NJ, with his wife, Anjinette, two daughters, Sofia and Mia and their black lab Bella. Coach Cronin teaches math at Suffern HS and for many years was the voice of the Mounties basketball games. He loves the NY Giants.

Assistant Coach: Wayne Poppe

Coach Poppe lives in Sloatsburg, NY, with his wife, Jessie, and four children, Kyle, Quinn, Hayden and Luke. Coach is an avid Green Bay Packers fan. His favorite Mountie Memory is beating North Rockland for the league championship in the 2003 Wood Bat Classic at RCC.

Varsity Pitching & Bench Coach: JR Randazzo

Coach Randazzo lives in Mahwah NJ, with his wife Brittany, and their dog Max. JR’s father Vinny is also an alumni of the program and was a pitcher as well. Hi favorite Mountie Memory is pitching in the 2008 section championship and then winning one as part of the coaching staff in 2019.

JV Head Coach: Nick Kulbaba

Coach Kulbaba lives in Suffern with the old man, and two pitbulls Boss and Sarge. Nick’s father Tony and brother Dom are also alumni of the program. His favorite Mountie Memory is the walk off home run at RCC to beat NR for the JV championship AND No hitting MAMO for 6 innings at home in the section semi finals, and beating them 3-0

JV Interim Head Coach: Chris Michie

Although Coach Michie hung up his spikes last season - we are fortunate to have him back as Coach Kulbaba works his way back from the DL. Coach Michie lives in Tuxedo, NY, with his wife, Christina, and puppy Gio. He retired from teaching & as Chair of the Science Dept at Suffern in 2021. Coach is an avid golfer.

JV Assistant Coach: Artie Connolly

Coach Connolly lives in Airmont with his wife Kristin, who is a teacher at Suffern HS, and his 3 boys Owen, Jack, and Ryan. Coach has been the Vice President of Suffern Little League and has dedicated 10 years to coaching the youth of our community.

Freshman Head Coach: Zach Saunders

Coach Saunders is our new Freshman Head Coach. He lives in Lodi, NJ with his girlfriend, Samantha and dogs Stella and Dakota. Zach played for Coach Cronin. His favorite Mountie Memory is winning the Rockland County JV Championship with Coach Sayre

Modified Head Coach: Tom Natoli

Coach Natoli currently resides in Hawthorne, NJ. He is also an assistant coach with the Suffern varsity and modified hockey programs. Coach also has been known to moonlight on several occasions for the NHL’s NY Rangers as their EBUG - Emergency Back-Up Goalie!!!

Modified Assistant Coach: Mike Sepulveda

Coach Sepulveda begins his coaching career back at Suffern working with his former coach, Tom Natoli. Mike enters his first season on the Mounties staff and is no stranger to the program. Mike was a huge part of the 2019 League, Sectional and Regional team. Coach Sepulveda lives in Suffern with his parents and two sisters, Julia and Natalie. Mike’s mom is an Italian teacher at SHS. (Grazie!!) Coach Sepulveda currently is in school working towards a degree in education.
